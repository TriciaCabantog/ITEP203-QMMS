My story

Hi I'm Tricia Cabantog currently 2nd year BSIT student from LSPU Sta. Cruz main campus. Since highschool I fell inlove in the art of photography. I have this urge feeling that I need to capture everything surround me nature, people, animals etc., So when I finally brought my first smartphone I was so excited also I decided that this is what I want to do in the future to be a photographer. That's where my passion to photography starts and I begin to explore and learn more about the beauty of taking photos.


For me taking photo is a way of preserving the memories we have because we might forget what happened when time passed but with the photo, is a proof or a reminder that those memories are exist. Photography is also a way of telling a story without using any words. Photography can be a bridge to everyone it connect us to our past they remind everything and help us to know who we are.

“The best thing about a picture is that it never changes, even when the people in it do.”
